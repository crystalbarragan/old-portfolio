Teaching Blog

I began teaching in 2010, and in this capacity I became involved in pedagogical discourse within the foreign language teaching community for several years.

In 2011, I published a website (senoritabarragan.com) to share my lesson ideas and instructional materials with other educators. By 2014, I was writing to a substantial audience of teachers, and I became actively involved in the growing discussion on distinctive methodologies in second language acquisition.

In these blog entries, I comment on many of my independent research efforts. Some entries are personal reflections, while others provide instructions and resources to teachers. Some of my most visited entries include:
